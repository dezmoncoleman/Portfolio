Budget Tracker

This is a front-end web application designed to help users manage their finances efficiently. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project is focused on creating interactive, user-friendly applications with a focus on functionality and design. The app dynamically updates the balance, helping users keep track of their financial status in real-time. Transactions can be categorized into groups like Food, Bills, Entertainment, etc., allowing for better organization and insights into spending habits. All transaction data is saved in the browser's local storage, ensuring that user information persists across sessions without needing a backend server.

About Me

I was born in 1993 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, a city known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and southern charm. Growing up, I was always fascinated by technology. I remember being a kid, sitting in front of the tv while my dad played Star Fox on the Nintendo 64, cheering him on like it was a football game. As technology advanced, so did my interest and curiosity, driving me to explore and learn more about the digital world.

Despite my love for technology, my professional journey initially took me down a different path. For the past 15 years, I immersed myself in the culinary arts, a field that offered its own unique challenges and rewards. Starting from the bottom, I worked my way up through various roles including server, line cook, and eventually, chef. Each position taught me valuable lessons about discipline, teamwork, and the importance of precision. In the high-pressure, fast-paced environment of a restaurant kitchen, I developed a robust set of skills. Time management became second nature as I balanced multiple tasks simultaneously, ensuring that each dish met high standards of quality and was served promptly. The ability to stay calm under pressure and think on my feet was crucial, especially during busy service hours when the kitchen was at its most chaotic. While I enjoyed the culinary world, my passion for technology never waned. I began to realize that the skills I had honed in the kitchen – time management, problem-solving, and working efficiently under pressure – were equally valuable in the tech industry. This realization sparked a desire to transition into a new career where I could merge my interests and skills. In 2023, I took the plunge and started teaching myself web development. I spent countless hours learning online, building small projects, and experimenting with different technologies. My dedication paid off when I enrolled in a bootcamp through Fullstack Academy in early 2024. This intensive program further solidified my knowledge and equipped me with a strong foundation in modern web development technologies. As I embark on this new journey in the tech field, I am excited about the endless possibilities and opportunities to grow. I am eager to apply my unique blend of culinary and technical skills to build innovative solutions and contribute to the ever-evolving digital landscape.
